Why Pueblo?




    An Affordability Crisis

    • 13% population increase in Colorado between 2010 and 2018
    • Cost of living in Denver is 12% higher than the national average
    • 23% cost of living increase in Denver in 2018, according to Forbes
    • 36% cost of living increase in Colorado Springs in 2018 (closest city to Pueblo), according to Forbes

    As populations grow and cost of living rises, people get priced out of large metropolises and look to move to more affordable cities

Why Pueblo?




    A Growing Economy

    • Pueblo has a growing healthcare industry
      • One of the fastest-growing industries in the world
      • Pueblo has x new hospitals, comapnies, etc
    • Remote work is changing where people need to live
      • As many as 30% of Americans work virtually
      • Hiring managers expect up to 38% of workers will be remote within the next decade

    Pueblo becomes an attractive option for people who can't afford to or don't want to live in larger cities

Why Pueblo?




    A Desirable Destination

    • Pueblo River Walk
      • A beautiful city-center tourism attraction
      • Bolsters local economy
      • Averaging 4.6 star online rating, over 1,300 reviews
    • Hyperloop
      • Route will travel directly between Denver and Pueblo
      • Less than an hour communte time
      • Anticipated operational in mid-2020s

    Home values in Pueblo are climbing steadily and are poised to increase dramatically as Pueblo continues to become a destination for both tourists and Coloradans.
